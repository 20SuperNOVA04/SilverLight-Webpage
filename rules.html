Rules

One Day Ban

• Harassment, homophobia, or any form of serious disrespect towards a member.

• Repeated petty griefing. (placing netherackk outside of bases, leaving creeper holes, etc.)

• Making a report on a player while having zero proof/evidence.

One Week Ban

• Breaking into a capital/town/FOB outside of war.

• Mass Stealing from chests outside of war.

• Breaking rules of war. (Explained in the war tab!)

Three Day Ban

• Using another persons farm without permission.

• Stealing from chests that are within a nations walls, no matter how small the amount was.

• Spawnkilling/targeting innocent players for no reason.

One Month Ban

• Hacking or Exploiting.

• Mass Griefing.

Season Ban

• Repeated offenses of serious rules

Rules may extend beyond this if necessary, please have common sense! If it feels wrong then think twice.

If any of this is confusing, please message a mod or assistant!
